Sluqe – AI-Powered Voice Knowledge Platform

Sluqe is an intelligent voice companion designed to help knowledge workers,

creators, and professionals transform everyday conversations into organized,

searchable, and actionable insights, fully powered by AI with zero manual effort. It

aims to be a "second brain" built for the speed of speech, focusing on voice-first

memory.

Key Features:

One-Tap Voice Recording (from meetings, calls, or ambient conversations)

AI Transcription \& Speaker Separation

Smart Summaries \& Topic Clustering

Memory Recall by Natural Language ("Ask Sluqe…")

Automatic Action Item Detection

Secure, Private AI (No human review)

Our Differentiator – AI Recall Engine:

Unlike most tools that stop at transcription, Sluqe builds a voice memory layer.

This allows users to ask natural language questions like "What did I say about the

pricing plan during last month's meeting with Alex?" and get instant answers with

contextual links across conversations, eliminating the need for folders, keywords,

or re-listening.
